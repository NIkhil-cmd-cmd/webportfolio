## Overview

In this project, I collaborated with a team of teens to design and develop a mental health app specifically for teenagers.

## Key Features

- **Journaling**: Developed a journaling feature for users to express their thoughts.
- **Community Support**: Created a community forum for users to connect.
- **Mental Health Chatbot**: Integrated a chatbot for mental health resources.
- **24/7 Support**: Established a support system connecting users with professionals.

## Technologies Used

- **React Native**: For building a cross-platform mobile app.
- **Firebase**: For secure backend infrastructure.
- **Machine Learning**: For developing a mental health chatbot.

## Challenges and Learnings

Ensuring the app was designed with the unique needs of teens involved user research and testing.

## Outcome

The Share-On app has been successfully implemented in several schools, providing a supportive community for teens.
