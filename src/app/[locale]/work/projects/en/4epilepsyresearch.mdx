## Overview

This project focuses on understanding epilepsy and improving treatment methods.

## Key Features

- **Data Collection**: Gathering data on epilepsy cases.
- **Analysis**: Analyzing data to identify patterns and treatment efficacy.

## Technologies Used

- **Statistical Software**: For data analysis.
- **Research Methodologies**: Various methods used in clinical research.

## Challenges and Learnings

Conducting research in a sensitive area like epilepsy requires careful consideration of ethical implications.

## Outcome

The research aims to contribute to better understanding and treatment of epilepsy.
