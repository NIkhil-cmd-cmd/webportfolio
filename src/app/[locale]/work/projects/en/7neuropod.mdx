## Overview

Neuropod is an innovative mastoid-based EEG device that I built from scratch, aimed at enhancing brainwave monitoring for various applications. This project involved extensive research and development, culminating in participation in multiple research and pitch competitions.

## Key Features

- **Custom Design**: The device was designed using Arduino components, allowing for flexibility and adaptability in EEG monitoring.
- **Research Competitions**: Neuropod has been showcased in various competitions, demonstrating its potential in the field of neuroscience and technology.
- **Pitch Competitions**: The project has also been presented in pitch competitions, highlighting its innovative approach and practical applications.

## Technologies Used

- **Arduino**: For the development and control of the EEG device.
- **EEG Sensors**: Utilized for capturing brainwave data.
- **Data Analysis Tools**: Employed for processing and analyzing the EEG data collected.

## Challenges and Learnings

Building Neuropod presented challenges such as ensuring accurate data capture and device reliability. These obstacles provided valuable insights into engineering principles and the importance of iterative testing.

## Outcome

The Neuropod project has not only advanced my understanding of EEG technology but has also inspired interest in neuroscience applications, paving the way for future innovations in brain-computer interfaces.
