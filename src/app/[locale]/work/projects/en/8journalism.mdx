## Overview

This program offers students the opportunity to engage in real-world scientific research.

## Key Features

- **Hands-On Experience**: Students work alongside professionals in the field.
- **Mentorship**: Each intern is paired with a mentor for guidance.
- **Research Projects**: Interns participate in ongoing research projects.

## Technologies Used

- **Laboratory Equipment**: Various tools and technologies used in research.
- **Data Analysis Software**: Tools for analyzing research data.

## Challenges and Learnings

Interns face challenges in adapting to real-world research environments.

## Outcome

Participants gain valuable experience and skills applicable to their future careers.
