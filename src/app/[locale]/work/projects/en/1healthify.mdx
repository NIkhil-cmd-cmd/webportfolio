## Overview

Over the past few months, I have been developing Healthify, a comprehensive wellness app designed to track and improve usersâ€™ physical and mental health through personalized plans, goal-setting features, and insightful analytics, with a focus on integration with Apple Health and existing iOS systems.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Health Plans**: Developed a system that generates customized health plans for users based on their fitness goals, diet preferences, and activity levels.
- **Progress Tracking**: Implemented a feature that allows users to log their daily activities, workouts, meals, and moods.
- **Integration with Apple HealthKit and Halo**: Integrated to automatically track physical activity and health metrics.
- **Machine Learning for Personalized Insights**: Leveraged CoreML to analyze user data and offer personalized recommendations.
- **Condition-Specific Health Plans**: Building out specific health plans tailored to various health conditions.
